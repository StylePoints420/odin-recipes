Chicken Parmesan in Cream Sauce

Description

This recipe is not your usual chicken Parmesan! Instead, chicken tenders are coated in a mixture of freshly grated Parmesan cheese and Italian-seasoned panko crumbs, fried to a golden brown, and finished in a lovely cream sauce.

A side of pasta would be good with this, or try mashed potatoes. A green vegetable and a salad will complete this dinner menu, nicely!

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds chicken breast tenders
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • ½ cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 1 ½ cups freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup Italian-seasoned panko bread crumbs
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • Parchment paper
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter, divided, or more as needed
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil, or more as needed
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • ½ cup dry white wine
  • 1 teaspoon dried Italian seasoning
  • ½ teaspoon red pepper flakes, or to taste
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • Chopped fresh parsley for garnish (Optional)
