Chili Garlic Oil

Description

Don't have an appetite? Sometimes, a little bit of spice is what our stomach needs and this Chili Garlic Oil is definitely the one for you!

Making it is easy! You only need these ingredients and steps to be followed down below!

Ingredients

  • Thai chili pepper
  • Garlic
  • Canola oil or olive oil
  • Star anise
  • Bay leaves
  • White vinegar
  • Hoisin sauce
  • Brown sugar
  • Salt
